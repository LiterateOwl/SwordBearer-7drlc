Attack list (in order of prioritiy):

Swipe: activates if there are 3 or more enemies in the tiles next to you. Deals 1 damage to all adjacient enemies

Sword Jump: activates if there is an enemy in the direction you moved, followed by an empty space and then another enemy. Does not stack.

Deals one damage to both enemies and moves you by two spaces in the direction you moved

Thrust: activates if there is an enemy in the direction you moved. Deals one damage to that enemy

List of enemies:

Chases the player and attacks if it is adjacent to the player

Chases the player until it is within three tiles and is either on the same x or y axis as the player, or on the same diagonal;
then charges along up to three tiles and damages the player if it is in the way
